# Sunk Cost



You are a miner working a company shaft deep underground. The quota resets each morning. The bills don't.

Food, shelter, tool repairs, taxes, and Maria's hospital bill, every single day. The company takes its cut before you see a cent. Meet your quota and earn a bonus. Miss it, and the debt grows.

Dig deeper. Find better ore. Upgrade your tools. Come back up before the oxygen runs out.
