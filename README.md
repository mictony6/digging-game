# Sunk Cost

> *Every swing of the pickaxe is for her.*

You are a miner working a company shaft deep underground. The quota resets each morning. The bills don't.

Food, shelter, tool repairs, taxes — and Maria's hospital bill, every single day. The company takes its cut before you see a cent. Meet your quota and earn a bonus. Miss it, and the debt grows.

Dig deeper. Find better ore. Upgrade your tools. Come back up before the oxygen runs out.

---

## Gameplay

Two interlocking loops drive the experience:

**The Grind**
`Dig → Meet Quota → Sell → Pay Expenses → Upgrade → Repeat`

Each day you descend into the mine. Break rock, manage your oxygen, and haul enough ore to meet the company's quota before heading back up. At day's end a full expense report lands on the table and whatever's left is yours to spend on upgrades that let you go deeper tomorrow.

**The Mystery**
`Dig → Discover Notes & Clues → Reach Hidden POIs → Uncover the Story → Earn Rewards`

The mine hides things the company doesn't want you to find. Scattered notes, strange markings, sealed-off passages. Follow the trail and piece together what really happened here — and why you're really down here.

---

## Features

- **First-person 3D mining** with responsive pickaxe, laser, and explosive tools
- **Durability system** — tools degrade with use, repair overnight, and can be upgraded across tier, power, and max durability
- **Oxygen management** — the deeper you go, the more carefully you breathe
- **Day/night economy** — itemised daily expense reports with growing quotas and tax
- **Shop & upgrade system** — buy consumables and permanently upgrade your equipment
- **Physics-based gadgets** — minecarts, lifts, rails, ladders, bombs
- **Hand-crafted levels** — a deliberate, authored mine with secrets worth finding
